Halts a program.

STOP

    ■ The STOP keyword also suspends trapping of events in these statements:

      ◄COM, ON COM►      ◄KEY, ON KEY►         ◄PEN, ON PEN►
      ◄PLAY, ON PLAY►    ◄STRIG, ON STRIG►     ◄TIMER, ON TIMER►

Example:
    FOR i% = 1 TO 10
        PRINT i%
        IF i% = 5 THEN STOP     'STOP pauses; F5 Continues.
    NEXT i%
